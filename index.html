Transforming Craniosynostosis Care Through AI-Driven Insights

CranioRate is a novel, machine-learning-powered platform that brings objectivity and precision to craniosynostosis assessment.

Learn More

An Objective, Data-Driven Approach to a Complex Condition

Craniosynostosis, the premature fusion of cranial sutures, affects approximately 1 in 2,500 infants. Clinical decisions about its severity and treatment have historically been based on subjective judgment, posing challenges for surgical planning and family counseling.

CranioRateTM was developed to meet this challenge head-on. As a novel, machine-learning-powered platform, our tools generate reproducible severity scores from imaging data (CT and 3D photography). This enables researchers and clinicians to better understand morphology, support surgical planning, counsel families, and evaluate outcomes over time with objective, precision data.

Why Choose CranioRate?

Our platform is built on four core principles:

Objective

Based on large datasets and machine learning.

Holistic

Incorporating multiple cranial types and severity indicators.

Easy to Use

Streamlined upload and scoring process.

Clinically Relevant

Built with input from practicing craniofacial teams.

Turning Insight Into Impact

CranioRate is built to serve the needs of a diverse set of users:

For Researchers

Access structured phenotype data for multicenter studies, registries, and advanced analytics.

For Surgeons

Use reproducible severity metrics to guide preoperative planning, intraoperative choices, and postoperative evaluation.

For Patients & Families

Empower informed decision-making through visualizations and understandable, data-driven communication.

For Outcomes Analysts

Track surgical and non-surgical treatment effectiveness over time with quantifiable metrics.

Example Applications

Real-world use cases for the CranioRate platform include:

Comparing preoperative and postoperative cranial morphology

Benchmarking surgical outcomes across centers

Supporting shared decision-making during patient consults

Publishing high-impact research using standardized, objective shape data

With each use, CranioRate helps transform individual care while building a collective understanding of craniosynostosis.
